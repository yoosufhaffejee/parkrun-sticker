# Sticker stat options (minimalist, share-worthy)

## Why hide time is valuable
Not every runner wants their finishing time on a public sticker. A great sticker should still feel celebratory even when time is private.

## Core design principle
Keep one **primary headline metric** and up to **2 compact secondary stats**. Let users toggle each stat on/off.

## High-value stat alternatives to finishing time

### 1) Achievement badge (recommended default)
- Examples: `Completed`, `New PB`, `Course PB`, `Comeback Run`, `Milestone`.
- Why people share it: identity + accomplishment without revealing pace.

### 2) Run streak / consistency
- Examples: `3-week streak`, `12 parkruns this year`, `5 in a row`.
- Why people share it: discipline narrative is highly social and motivating.

### 3) Milestone progress
- Examples: `Run #24 (1 to milestone)`, `Volunteer #9`.
- Why people share it: creates anticipation and invites community support.

### 4) Relative result (privacy-friendly)
- Examples: `Top 35% today`, `+42 places vs last run`.
- Why people share it: improvement context without exposing exact time.

### 5) Course/community context
- Examples: `1st time at this course`, `Tourist stop #7`, `Parkrun #56 overall`.
- Why people share it: story value and travel/community signal.

### 6) Feel-good wellness angle
- Examples: `5K done before 9am`, `Strong finish`, `Movement > perfection`.
- Why people share it: emotional resonance and inclusive messaging.

## Suggested stat picker model (simple)

### Headline slot (pick one)
- Finishing time
- Achievement badge
- Milestone status

### Secondary slot A (pick one)
- Position or percentile
- Streak/consistency metric
- Course context

### Secondary slot B (optional)
- Personal note/tagline
- Weather/conditions icon + short label
- Volunteer/crew acknowledgement

## Smart presets to avoid style bloat
Use content presets instead of extra visual controls:

1. **Performance**: Time + place + PB delta
2. **Progress**: Milestone + streak + total runs
3. **Story**: Course context + personal note + date
4. **Privacy-first**: Achievement badge + consistency + milestone

## Minimal UI addition proposal
- Add a small `Show on sticker` section with toggles/chips for each stat.
- Keep typography/layout unchanged; only swap content blocks.
- If too many stats are selected, auto-limit to 3 visible blocks and show a short helper text.

## Priority rollout suggestion
1. Add **Hide time** toggle.
2. Add **Achievement badge**, **Streak**, **Milestone progress** as first alternatives.
3. Add **presets** to make selection one-tap.

This gives users meaningful control and better social shareability while preserving a clean, minimalist sticker style.
